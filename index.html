How it works

Three steps. Twenty seconds. Every night.

Define your tracks

Pick 3 to 5 things that make a good week for you. Not goals. Not habits. Just the things that matter.

Check in each night

For each track, tap one of three states: moved forward, held steady, or drifted. That's it.

See the patterns

Over days and weeks, Tally reveals whether your life is actually moving — without judgment.
